----------------------------------------------------
1. Client (Customer)
----------------------------------------------------
Use Cases:
- Browse boutiques and products
- Search/filter products
- Add products to cart
- Place orders
- Track order status
- Manage profile and addresses
- Rate and review products/boutiques
- Wishlist/Favorites
- View order history and reorder
- Receive notifications (order status, promotions, new arrivals)
- Secure payments with multiple options
- Contact boutique owners or customer support (chat/support)
- Personalized product recommendations
- Apply promo codes and discounts
- Participate in loyalty/rewards program
- Share products/boutiques on social media
- Compare products
- Initiate return/refund requests

AI Features:
- Personalized product recommendations using AI based on browsing and purchase history
- Smart search and filtering with natural language understanding
- Visual search: Upload a photo to find similar products
- Chatbot support for instant help and order tracking
- Dynamic pricing suggestions (showing best deals or price drops)

----------------------------------------------------
2. Delivery Person
----------------------------------------------------
Use Cases:
- View assigned deliveries
- Update delivery status (picked up, on the way, delivered)
- View delivery history
- Contact customer or support
- Apply to become a delivery person (visitor submits application and documents)
- Manage profile and availability (set working hours, mark as available/unavailable)
- Receive notifications for new delivery assignments or updates
- View earnings and payment history
- Rate and review delivery experience (feedback from/to customers)
- Access navigation/maps for delivery routes
- Report issues or incidents during delivery
- Access training materials or guidelines

AI Features:
- Optimized delivery route planning using AI for fastest/most efficient routes
- Predictive workload management (suggesting best times to work)
- Automatic incident detection (AI detects delays or issues and notifies support)

----------------------------------------------------
3. Admin
----------------------------------------------------
Use Cases:
- Manage users (clients, boutique owners, delivery personnel, managers)
- Approve/reject boutique registrations
- Manage subscriptions and payments
- View analytics and reports
- Manage platform settings
- Manage roles and permissions for all users
- Moderate and resolve disputes (orders, reviews, etc.)
- Send platform-wide notifications or announcements
- Manage content (banners, promotions, FAQs, terms & conditions)
- Monitor system health and logs (error tracking, uptime)
- Export data (users, orders, sales, etc.) for reporting
- Manage and review feedback/complaints from users
- Set and manage commission rates or fees for boutiques/deliveries
- Manage tax and invoicing settings
- View and manage refund/return requests
- Impersonate users for troubleshooting/support
- Manage multi-language/localization settings
- Integrate and manage third-party services (payment gateways, SMS, etc.)

----------------------------------------------------
4. Boutique Owner
----------------------------------------------------
Use Cases:
- Register and subscribe to a plan
- Set up and manage boutique page (vitrine)
- Add/edit/remove products
- Manage orders (view, accept, prepare)
- View sales and analytics
- Communicate with customers
- Manage boutique profile and branding (logo, description, banners)
- Manage working hours and delivery options
- Set up and manage promotions, discounts, and coupons
- Manage inventory and stock alerts
- Receive notifications for new orders, low stock, or customer messages
- View and respond to customer reviews/ratings
- Access support or help center
- Manage return/refund requests
- Schedule product launches or flash sales

AI Features:
- AI-driven inventory management (predicting stock needs, auto-reordering)
- Dynamic pricing suggestions based on demand and competition
- AI-powered customer segmentation for targeted promotions

----------------------------------------------------
5. Delivery Manager
----------------------------------------------------
Use Cases:
- Assign deliveries to delivery personnel
- Track delivery status and performance
- Manage delivery personnel
- View delivery analytics
- Receive notifications for new orders requiring delivery assignment
- Manage delivery zones/areas and optimize routes
- View and manage delivery schedules and shifts
- Handle delivery issues or incidents (report, resolve, reassign)
- Communicate with delivery personnel and customers
- Approve or reject delivery personnel applications
- Monitor real-time location of delivery personnel
- Generate and export delivery performance reports
- Set delivery policies (timing, fees, etc.)
- Access support or help center

AI Features:
- AI-based delivery assignment (auto-assigning orders to the best-suited delivery person)
- Predictive demand analytics (forecasting busy times/areas)
- Performance analytics with AI-driven insights and suggestions
- Incident prediction and prevention (AI flags potential delivery issues)

----------------------------------------------------
Use Cases:
- Oversee all boutiques on the platform

- View overall sales and performance analytics

1. Delivery Workflow (Delivery Person/Agent)
Automate assignment: When an order is ready, the system (agent) assigns it to the best available delivery person.
Step-by-step process:
Receive delivery assignment
Accept/reject assignment
Navigate to boutique
Pick up package
Deliver to client
Confirm delivery (with photo/signature)
Report incident if needed
